# SummerFleur's Better Hats

English | [简体中文](README_zh.md)

In the vanilla game, hats are often nothing more than expensive decorations. You spend hard-earned
money or complete challenges to unlock them, only to find they serve no real purpose—a bit of a
letdown, isn’t it? So I created this mod to give almost every hat a unique effect.

In this mod, hat effects are divided into four main categories:

1. Utility Buffs: Provides various powerful buffs to assist with daily chores and adventures.
2. Mechanic Tweaks: Modifies game rules to offer unexpected conveniences or brand-new gameplay 
   styles.
3. Social Interactions: Wearing specific hats unlocks unique dialogue with marriageable villagers
   and grants extra friendship points.
4. Just for Fun: Purely for fun, adding a touch of humor and surprise to your farm life.

Hope this mod brings a fresh and colorful experience to your time in Stardew Valley!

This mod requires [Better Hats API (BHA)](../BetterHatsAPI), and BHA provides a comprehensive guide
book menu that can show all the effect of hats in one area, so if you want know more about the
effect of hats, just press the hot key (By default is `Ctrl + Alt + H`), and you can see them in the
menu conveniently.
